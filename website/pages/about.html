MyPass

A simple app to share secrets

MyPass allows users to share secrets in a secure and ephemeral way

Sharing one-time URLs is much safer than sending your sensitive data in emails, texts or instant message

Secrets are always encrypted and decryption keys are never stored on MyPass

Data stored on MyPass should be considered ephemeral at all times


How do I use MyPass?

Input your secret, select the expiration of the secert and click "Generate" to create a one-time use URL for secret retrieval

Share this URL with your intended recipient

Secrets are immediately purged upon retrieval or expiration, whichever happens first


Who can retrieve my secret?

Anyone within posession of the URL can retrieve your secret. First come first serve


Is there any way to retrieve the secret apart from the URL?

Decrypting secrets outside of the one-time URL process is not possible

Keys are contained only within the URL and stored no where else
